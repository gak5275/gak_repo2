Write a premise for a musical about a digital humanities college class.
"Code and Chorus: A Digital Humanities Musical" follows a group of students enrolled in a cutting-edge college course, exploring the intersection of technology and the arts. As they delve into the world of digital humanities, they discover the power of collaboration, creativity, and the human spirit. Through a mix of tech-savvy numbers and soaring musical numbers, the students come to realize that the future of the arts lies in the fusion of technology and human emotion. In the end, they graduate with a newfound appreciation for the limitless potential of digital humanities, ready to change the world with their passion and skills.
