Write a premise for a drama show about a digital humanities college class.
"Digital Renaissance" follows a group of diverse and ambitious students in a competitive digital humanities college class, where they learn about the intersection of technology and the arts. As they delve into the world of artificial intelligence, virtual reality, and digital preservation, they struggle with the ethics and societal implications of their work. As they navigate the cutthroat academia and their own personal challenges, they form unexpected alliances and relationships that challenge their beliefs and shape their futures. The series explores the blurred lines between humanity and technology in a rapidly changing world.
