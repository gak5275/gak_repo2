Write a premise for a holiday movie about a digital humanities college class.
"The Digital Holiday" is a heartwarming holiday movie that takes place at a digital humanities college. The film follows a group of students in the class as they embark on a journey to save their beloved professor's virtual reality Christmas program, which has gone haywire. With the help of a friendly AI and a cast of quirky characters, the students must work together to fix the program and ensure that the virtual Christmas celebration goes off without a hitch. Along the way, the students learn important lessons about friendship, teamwork, and the true meaning of the holiday season. The film is a blend of humor, adventure, and holiday spirit as the students experience the joys of the virtual holiday world and discover the power of technology to bring people together. "The Digital Holiday" is a festive and uplifting movie that celebrates the magic of the holidays and the endless possibilities of technology.
