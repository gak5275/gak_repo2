Write a premise for a cyberpunk movie about a digital humanities college class.
"The Net Runners" is a gritty cyberpunk movie that takes place at a digital humanities college. The film follows a group of students in a virtual reality class who stumble upon a powerful piece of technology that could change the balance of power in the city's criminal underworld. With the help of a cunning hacker and a rogue AI, the students must navigate the dangerous world of the city's darknet, evading dangerous gangs and corrupt corporations to uncover the truth behind the technology. The film blends elements of technology, cyberpunk genre tropes, and suspense as the students use their technical skills, street smarts, and bravery to outwit the bad guys and save the city from certain destruction. "The Net Runners" is a thrilling and action-packed cyberpunk movie that explores the dangers of technology, the power of friendship, and the courage to do what is right in a world where nothing is as it seems.
