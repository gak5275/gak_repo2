Write a premise for a time travel movie about a digital humanities college class.
"The Time Navigators" is a mind-bending time travel movie that takes place at a digital humanities college. The film follows a group of students in a virtual reality class who stumble upon a powerful piece of technology that allows them to travel through time. With the help of a mysterious professor and a rogue AI, the students embark on a journey through history, visiting key moments in time and using their technical skills to change the course of history for the better. But as they travel further and further back in time, the students soon realize that their actions have unintended consequences, and they must race against the clock to correct their mistakes and prevent a catastrophic chain reaction that could destroy the fabric of time itself. "The Time Navigators" is a thrilling and action-packed time travel movie that explores the power of technology, the consequences of our actions, and the importance of making the right decisions in the face of an uncertain future.
