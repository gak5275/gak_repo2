Write a premise for a music video about a digital humanities college class.
"The Digital Beat" is a music video that follows a group of students in a digital humanities college class as they explore the world of music and technology. The students, each with their own unique style and musical background, collaborate on a project to create a cutting-edge virtual reality concert experience. The video showcases the students' creative process as they design, code, and perform an electrifying virtual performance that blends music, technology, and art. With stunning visuals, dynamic choreography, and a pulsing soundtrack, the music video captures the energy and excitement of a new generation of digital artists pushing the boundaries of what is possible. The video celebrates the limitless potential of technology and the power of music to bring people together.
