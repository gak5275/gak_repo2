Write a premise for a video game about a digital humanities college class.
"Code Quest" is an action-packed adventure video game that takes place at a digital humanities college. The player takes on the role of a student in the class, tasked with completing a series of coding challenges and exploring the virtual world. As they progress through the game, they encounter a diverse cast of characters, including eccentric professors, rival classmates, and fantastical creatures, all while uncovering the secrets of the digital realm. The game blends elements of puzzle-solving, exploration, and action as the player navigates a variety of environments, from virtual reality simulations to dark and dangerous cyber spaces. Along the way, the player must use their coding skills and quick thinking to overcome obstacles and complete the ultimate challenge: to save the digital world from a sinister force that threatens to destroy it. The game celebrates the power of technology and the thrill of the unknown.
