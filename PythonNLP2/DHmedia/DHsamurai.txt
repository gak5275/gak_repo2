Write a premise for a samurai movie about a digital humanities college class.
"The Digital Ronin" is a timeless samurai movie that takes place at a digital humanities college. The film follows a group of students in a virtual reality class who are suddenly transported to ancient Japan and find themselves in the middle of a war between rival factions. With the help of a seasoned samurai and a wise-cracking AI, the students must use their technical skills and bravery to survive in the samurai world, and ultimately, help bring peace to the land. The film blends elements of technology, samurai genre tropes, and humor as the students take on fearsome enemies, participate in epic battles, and learn the ways of the samurai. "The Digital Ronin" is a thrilling and action-packed samurai movie that celebrates the power of technology, the strength of friendship, and the courage to stand up for what is right.
