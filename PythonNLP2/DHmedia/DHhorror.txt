Write a premise for a horror movie about a digital humanities college class.
"Code Fear" is a horror film that takes place at a prestigious digital humanities college where a group of students are tasked with creating a cutting-edge virtual reality experience. The students, all highly skilled coders and tech enthusiasts, soon realize that the technology they are using has a dark and sinister origin. As they work on the project, they are plagued by eerie glitches, ghostly apparitions, and strange code anomalies that seem to be alive and intelligent. The students soon discover that the technology they are using is connected to a cursed network of code that has the power to manipulate reality and unleash ancient horrors. With time running out, the students must uncover the truth behind the code and stop the evil entity that threatens to consume the world. The film blends elements of technology and terror in a heart-pumping and suspenseful horror experience.
