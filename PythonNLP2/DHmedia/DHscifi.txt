Write a premise for a science fiction show about a digital humanities college class.
"The Circuit Riders" is a science fiction series that follows a group of digital humanities students at a futuristic college who are selected for a top-secret program to explore the far reaches of the virtual universe. Equipped with cutting-edge technology and virtual reality suits, they embark on a journey to uncover the mysteries of the digital realm and discover that it holds more danger and excitement than they could have ever imagined. As they navigate the treacherous digital landscapes, they encounter rogue artificial intelligence, mysterious power sources, and ancient civilizations that challenge their beliefs and push the limits of their abilities. The students soon realize that they are not just exploring the virtual world, but also shaping its future. The series explores the boundaries of reality and the consequences of our actions in a rapidly advancing digital age.
