Write a premise for a movie about a digital humanities college class.
"The Digital Dreamers" is a coming-of-age drama film that follows a group of students in a digital humanities college class as they work on their capstone project. The project, a cutting-edge virtual reality experience, requires the students to collaborate and push the limits of their abilities. As they delve deeper into the project, they face personal struggles, relationship drama, and ethical dilemmas that threaten to derail their dreams and their future. With the help of their dedicated professor and the support of each other, the students must overcome their challenges and find the courage to pursue their passions in a rapidly changing world. The film explores the complexities of technology, the power of friendship, and the importance of following one's dreams.
