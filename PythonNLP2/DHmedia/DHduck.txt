Write a premise for a show about a digital humanities college class starring DIGIT duck.
"Code Quack: A Digital Humanities Adventure" is a fun and educational show that follows the adventures of DIGIT Duck, a tech-savvy duck who enrolls in a digital humanities college class. Along with his classmates, DIGIT explores the world of technology and the arts, learning about coding, data visualization, and digital storytelling. With his quick wit and contagious enthusiasm, DIGIT makes learning about digital humanities exciting and accessible for audiences of all ages. Whether he's working on group projects, solving coding challenges, or taking in guest lectures, DIGIT's boundless energy and curious spirit make him the perfect guide to this fascinating field. Join DIGIT and his classmates for a fun and enlightening journey into the world of digital humanities!
