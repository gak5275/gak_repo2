Write a premise for a thriller movie about a digital humanities college class.
"The Code Breach" is a gripping thriller movie that takes place at a digital humanities college. The film follows a group of students in a cutting-edge virtual reality class who discover that their project has been hacked by a mysterious intruder. As they delve deeper into the breach, the students uncover a dark and sinister plot that threatens to not only destroy their project, but also the very fabric of reality. With the help of a tech-savvy professor and a brilliant computer programmer, the students must race against time to stop the hacker and prevent a catastrophic event that could have devastating consequences for the world. The film blends elements of technology and suspense as the students use their skills and determination to uncover the truth and save the day. "The Code Breach" explores the dangers of technology and the power of the human spirit to overcome even the greatest challenges.
