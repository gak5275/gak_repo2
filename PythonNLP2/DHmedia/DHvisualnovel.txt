Write a premise for a visual novel about a digital humanities college class.
"The Digital Diaries" is a visual novel that follows the story of a student in a digital humanities college class. The player takes on the role of the protagonist, navigating their way through the complexities of college life, relationships, and the digital world. As they work on a virtual reality project with their classmates, they encounter a range of characters and make important choices that shape the story. The player must balance their studies, relationships, and personal goals as they explore the virtual world and discover its hidden secrets. Along the way, they face moral dilemmas, emotional challenges, and difficult decisions that test their character and determine the outcome of the story. The visual novel combines elements of romance, drama, and mystery as it explores the many facets of the digital age and the power of technology to shape our lives and our world.
