Write a premise for a fantasy show about a digital humanities college class.
"Code Magic" is a fantasy series that follows a group of students enrolled in a prestigious digital humanities program at a mysterious college hidden in a parallel world. The students, each gifted in their own unique way, are tasked with solving ancient and impossible coding puzzles, unlocking hidden knowledge and powers that have been locked away for centuries. As they progress through the program, they discover that their abilities are rooted in magic and that the technology they have been studying is powered by it. With the help of their eccentric professor and a colorful cast of fellow students, they must use their newfound powers to defend the college and their world from dark forces threatening to destroy all that they hold dear. The series blends elements of magic and technology in a thrilling adventure through a fantastical realm.
