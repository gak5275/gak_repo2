Write a premise for a soap opera about a digital humanities college class.
"The Human Code: A Digital Humanities Soap Opera" follows a group of students and their professors at a prestigious college as they navigate the challenges and drama of a digital humanities course. With a mix of diverse backgrounds and conflicting personalities, the students form alliances, engage in romantic relationships, and compete for the top grade in the class. As they delve into the world of technology and the arts, they find themselves at the center of ethical debates and personal struggles. With each new assignment, the stakes become higher, and the drama intensifies. In the end, the students must come together to face the consequences of their choices and learn the true meaning of collaboration, creativity, and the power of technology in the humanities.
