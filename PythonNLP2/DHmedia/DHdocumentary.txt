Write a premise for a documentary about a digital humanities college class.
"The Future Builders" is a thought-provoking documentary that takes a closer look at a digital humanities college class. The film follows a group of students as they navigate the challenges and opportunities of this cutting-edge field, exploring the ways in which technology and the humanities intersect and the impact they have on the world. Through interviews with leading experts in the field, personal stories from the students, and a behind-the-scenes look at their digital humanities projects, "The Future Builders" provides a unique perspective on the future of education, technology, and society. This inspiring and eye-opening documentary is a must-see for anyone interested in technology, the humanities, and the future of our world.
