Write a premise for a superhero movie about a digital humanities college class.
"The Code Warriors" is a high-octane superhero movie that follows a group of students in a digital humanities college class as they uncover their extraordinary abilities. The students, all skilled coders and tech enthusiasts, discover that they have been gifted with special powers that are connected to their abilities to manipulate technology. With the help of their eccentric professor and a mysterious mentor, the students must come together to form a team of superheroes and use their powers to save the world from a powerful and dangerous cyber criminal. The film blends elements of action, drama, and comedy as the students learn to harness their powers, navigate their newfound roles as superheroes, and overcome the challenges of college life and the digital world. "The Code Warriors" celebrates the power of technology, the strength of friendship, and the courage to stand up and make a difference.
