"The Digital Humanities Unplugged" is a podcast that takes listeners inside a cutting-edge digital humanities college class. From coding projects and group assignments to guest lectures and class discussions, listeners will get an in-depth look at the challenges and rewards of digital humanities education. Join us for an exciting and thought-provoking journey into the world of digital humanities!
